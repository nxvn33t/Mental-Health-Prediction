Mending Minds

Mental illness is a health problem that undoubtedly impacts emotions, reasoning, and social interaction of a person. These issues have shown that mental illness gives serious consequences across societies and demands new strategies for prevention and intervention. To accomplish these strategies, early detection of mental health is an essential procedure.

Our aim is to help achieve the mental well-being of an individual by studying the various patterns observed in their thinking process. We aim to improve through experience by using advanced statistical and probabilistic techniques.Our goal is to provide a tool for predicting mental health. Many researchers could even acquire important information from the data, provide personalized experiences.
